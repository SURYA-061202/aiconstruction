# Project Vision: BuildGuard

## The Challenge
Construction projects often face delays and safety risks due to manual validation of architectural drawings. Compliance with Building Regulations (like Approved Documents in the UK) is complex and prone to human error.

## Our Solution
**BuildGuard** leverages the **RCK Engine** to provide a "Build-Regs-as-Code" validation layer. By mapping physical drawing data to a **Non-Scalar Validation Graph (NSVG)**, we provide architects and site managers with instant, trustworthy compliance feedback.

### Key Pillars

1.  **Automated Validation**
    Automate the tedious checks of staircase dimensions, fire compartmentation, and material suitability.
    
2.  **Safety First (Building Regs Compliance)**
    Built-in logic for UK/Global standards, specifically using the `aec-drawings-v1` plugin suite.

3.  **Zero Hallucination Reporting**
    Unlike standard LLMs, the RCK Engine ensures that every validation result is traceable back to the source drawing and specific regulatory clauses.

4.  **Premium Experience**
    A sleek, modern interface that makes complex data digestible through interactive compliance dashboards.

## Target Audience
- **Architects**: Instant feedback during the design phase.
- **Building Control Officers**: Streamlined auditing and approval workflows.
- **Construction Managers**: Real-time safety monitoring on-site.
